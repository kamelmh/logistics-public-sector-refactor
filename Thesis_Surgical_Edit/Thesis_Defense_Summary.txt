PROBLEM STATEMENT | إشكالية البحث
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Traditional inventory management at the Directorate of Education suffers from:
- Manual tracking leading to stockouts (نقص المخزون)
- Overstocking due to poor forecasting (تراكم المخزون)
- Lack of decision support tools for administrative staff
- No automated alerts for replenishment

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PROPOSED SOLUTION | الحل المقترح
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
A pure VBA/Excel Decision Support System with:
- Automated ROP and EOQ calculations (حساب نقطة إعادة الطلب والكمية الاقتصادية)
- Stock level monitoring with visual alerts (مراقبة مستويات المخزون)
- PDF report generation for administrative reporting (تقارير PDF)
- Zero external dependencies (NO Python, NO Flask, NO JSON, NO APIs)
- Fully compliant with CNEPD guidelines (متوافق مع توجيهات CNEPD)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
DEFENSE ANSWERS | إجابات محتملة للدفاع
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Q: Why use VBA instead of Python/Flask?
A: CNEPD guidelines require public sector solutions to be:
   - Maintainable by administrative staff (سهولة الصيانة)
   - Free from external dependencies (بدون تبعيات خارجية)
   - Compatible with standard office software (إكسل متوفر في جميع الإدارات)
   - VBA is embedded in Excel - no installation required

Q: How is data stored without a database?
A: Using Excel worksheets as "السجل الرقمي" (Digital Registry):
   - tbl_Stock: Current inventory levels
   - tbl_Transactions: Stock movements (in/out)
   - tbl_Settings: ROP/EOQ parameters
   - All processed via VBA modules - no external database needed

Q: What about scalability?
A: For Directorate-level operations (500-1000 items):
   - Excel handles 1M+ rows efficiently
   - VBA processing is sufficient for daily transactions
   - PDF reports provide archival records
   - System designed for administrative (not enterprise) scale
