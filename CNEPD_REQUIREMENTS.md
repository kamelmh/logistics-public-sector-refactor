# CNEPD Requirements Tracking

## General Guidelines
- **Real Data:** Required for the case study to demonstrate practical application.
- **Privacy:** Anonymize sensitive personal or financial information.
- **Core Focus:** Professional application of theoretical concepts to a public sector problem.

## Key Requirements
- **Practicality:** A fully functional software solution implemented in pure VBA.
- **Compliance:** 
    - Strict adherence to structural hierarchy (`فصل` $\rightarrow$ `مبحث` $\rightarrow$ `مطلب`).
    - Correct terminology (e.g., السجل الرقمي instead of Database).
- **Demonstrable Results:** Evidence of the system's ability to manage:
    - Annual Demand (D)
    - Safety Stock (SS)
    - Reorder Point (ROP)
    - Economic Order Quantity (Q*)

## Case Study Details
- **Institution:** Directorate of Education, El Bayadh.
- **Item:** Toner G030 (ART-001).
